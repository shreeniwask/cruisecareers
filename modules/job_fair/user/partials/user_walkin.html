FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS (FAQs)

What is a Virtual Career Fair (VCF)?

Cruisecareers.in's VCF is a recruitment event that brings together cruise ship career aspirants-you, and the Cruisecareers recruitment team -us, online in a face-to-face video setting. The VCF provides you with an opportunity to explore an exciting cruise ship career from the comforts of your home without visiting our office. All you need to do is, log-on to www.cruisecareers.in and look for the VCF tab on the home page to schedule an appointment.

Who is eligible to attend the Cruisecareers VCF?

Our minimum requirement to apply for an entry-level position is 18 years with a 3-year hotel school diploma or 21 years with one year of current work experience in any hospitality sector. Fluency in spoken English and 10 std. pass is mandatory. For more info, why not register and look at job descriptions to see if you qualify?

Where and when do the VCFs hosted by Cruisecareers take place?

Regularly, in fact. The VCFs are always hosted on the Cruisecareers.in website and you can access it from your computer, tablet or smartphone. Look out for the schedule in VCF tab on our homepage to go to the event page where you can register and attend the fair.

What are the cruise ship brands that you recruit for during the VCF?

Cruisecareers.in is the career portal of Carnival Support Services India Pvt. Ltd. We recruit for some of the best cruise ship brands in the world such as P&O Cruises, Princess Cruises, P&O Cruises Australia, Holland America Line, Cunard, Seabourn, Costa and Carnival Cruise Line. You can check our profile here.

How will I know when a VCF is scheduled?

We will be posting updates on our social media pages (Facebook, Instagram, Twitter and LinkedIn) and our website cruisecareers.in on the VCF schedule. Make sure you 'Like' and 'Follow' our social media pages to keep yourself updated.

Do I need to pay any service charges or fees to attend the VCF?

All recruitment is free. You do not need to pay any fees or service charges to attend the VCF.

What do I need to do to participate in the VCF?

To participate in the VCF, you have to sign-up and register yourself on cruisecareers.in to create a profile. An updated profile will help our recruitment team have a mutually beneficial interaction with you at the VCF. Therefore, we recommend that you complete your profile including uploading your education and work experience certificates.

Is registration compulsory for the VCF?

We recommend that you register for the VCF before the actual event. Your advantage: You can go through the job description and apply for suitable jobs. It also helps us to better plan the event and give you the best possible experience.

Can I update my CV after registering?

Yes, you can update it on your Cruisecareers profile page.

What personal information about me will Cruisecareers have access to?

We take your personal information quite seriously and will only have access to information that you chose to provide during the registration process. We will also be able to save a recording of the video interaction that they have with you for future reference.

Is uploading my documents into my Cruisecareers profile all I need to do to participate?

Uploading your personal, educational and professional documents will help our recruiters to know a little a bit about you before they speak to you. Check for profile completeness on the profile page. The higher the rating the more information our recruiters have about you to take a decision.

Do I need to cancel my registration if I find myself unable to attend the event?

We would appreciate if you could cancel the meeting, as it would mean someone else could use the vacated slot. You can cancel your registration on the event page.

I have a video meeting scheduled with you - what should I be wearing?

You should be dressed as if you were going on an interview. Make that first impression a good one! We also recommend you check what is visible behind you so that there is nothing to distract the recruiter. Make sure to speak loud and clear, preferably in a quiet surrounding.

Do I need to schedule a time slot for each position I have applied for?

You can apply for as many positions as you want. However, you need to schedule a slot only once. The recruiter will already have the details of the positions you have applied for and will screen you accordingly.

I am unable to log-in to the VCF at the scheduled time. Can I rebook a slot?

Yes you may. Please look out for an alternate date in the VCF tab and schedule a fresh slot.

Can I change my meeting slot if I am unable to make it for the earlier booked slot?

We would appreciate very much if you are able to meet us at the booked timing. However, if you are unable to meet us, then you will have to cancel your earlier appointment and book a fresh slot. After registering and updating your profile, please visit the VCF page on cruisecareers.in and schedule a meeting with our recruitment team. On the day of the event, please visit cruisecareers.in a few minutes before your scheduled meeting to view some interesting videos prior to speaking with our recruiter.

Is the virtual interview room at the VCF open continuously during the fair or only during assigned times?

The virtual interview room will be open for pre-defined hours during the VCF. You will see the time slots once you click the 'Schedule Meeting' button, from where you can select the pre-defined time slot, when logged into your account.

Do actual interviews take place during the fair?

This is completely up to the recruiter. We would like to use the VCF for a screening round before you are shortlisted for a position and invited to a personal interview. Speaking to the recruiter on this platform is the first step in potentially securing a job offer.

If there are a number of participants trying to connect at the same time, will there be a waiting list in order of who joined the session first?

There is no waiting list, just a natural flow of participants. Recruiters may have a lot of traffic (just as in a live fair) and cannot reach everyone immediately. Remember that patience is highly appreciated by the recruiters and the right job is worth the wait!

If I'm shortlisted at the VCF, will I be offered a letter of employment?

More often than not, after speaking to you and looking at your profile, our recruiters will be able to shortlist you for a position and invite you for a personal interview. However, you are free to apply for one job or multiple jobs - it doesn't matter.

I was not shortlisted for a personal interview. Can I try again?

If you have not been shortlisted for a position, please wait for six months before applying again. We recommend that you equip yourself with skills or competencies in the interim period to make yourself suitable for the applied position. Good Luck!

What will I need before I meet the recruiter at the VCF?

You will need a webcam and an updated version of either Google Chrome, Internet Explorer or Firefox to participate in video chats or receive their audio/video broadcasts.

I am having difficulty connecting to the recruiter. What should I do?

Make sure you are using an updated version of the browser (recommended - Google Chrome/ Firefox). Please add the Virtual Career Fair URL to your list of trusted sites. Safari and Internet Explorer is also supported, however, you must update to the latest version. Please ensure that you have a good internet connection.
